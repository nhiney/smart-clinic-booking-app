### Article 1. Data We Collect / Điều 1. Dữ liệu chúng tôi thu thập
To provide a safe and personalized medical experience, we collect data groups based on your role:

**1.1. For Patients / Đối với Người dùng Bệnh nhân:**
- **Basic Identity:** Mobile number (for OTP verification), Full name, Date of birth, Gender.
- **Medical Data (Sensitive):** Booking history, reported symptoms, medical records, test results, and prescriptions assigned by doctors through the platform.

**1.2. For Doctors/Partners / Đối với Y Bác sĩ và Đối tác:**
- **Identity & Contact:** Email, Phone number, Full name.
- **Verification Data (KYC):** Photos of National ID/Passport, Medical practice certificate, Medical degree, and Workplace/Hospital information. (These files are uploaded via absolute secure encrypted streams).

**1.3. Technical Data / Dữ liệu Kỹ thuật:**
- Device information, IP address, and Audit Logs for debugging and fraud prevention.

---

### Article 2. Purpose of Use / Điều 2. Mục đích sử dụng dữ liệu
We comply with the **"Principle of Least Privilege"**. Your data is only used for:
- **Service Provision:** Booking appointments, managing health records, and matching Patients with the right Specialists.
- **Verification & Security:** Strict KYC for Doctors to ensure 100% legality.
- **Notifications:** Appointment confirmations, medication reminders, or account approval alerts.
- **Legal Compliance:** Permanent Audit Logs for account approvals to meet inspection requirements.

---

### Article 3. Data Sharing / Điều 3. Chia sẻ và Tiết lộ dữ liệu
We commit **NOT TO SELL, RENT, or EXCHANGE** your personal data or medical records with any third party for commercial advertising. Data is only shared in strict contextual cases:
- **Patient & Doctor:** Doctors only access a Patient's records IF AND ONLY IF that Patient has booked and been assigned to that specific Doctor.
- **Facility Administrators:** Access only to data within their scope (Multi-tenant isolation).
- **Cloud Providers:** Data is stored and processed via Google Cloud & Firebase (ISO 27001, SOC 2 compliant).
- **Legal Requirements:** Disclosure upon written request by competent State Authorities under Vietnamese law.

---

### Article 4. Security Commitment / Điều 4. Cam kết Bảo mật (Zero Trust)
ICare applies the highest **Zero Trust Security** architecture:
- **Encryption:** All ID photos, degrees, and medical records are stored in locked Cloud Storage partitions with Security Rules.
- **Contextual Access (ABAC & RBAC):** No broad permissions. Users cannot cross-access others' data.
- **System Immutability:** All approval actions are recorded in Immutable Audit Logs.

---

### Article 5. Data Subject Rights / Điều 5. Quyền của Chủ thể dữ liệu
Under Decree 13/2023/ND-CP, you have all rights to:
- Access and Edit your personal info directly on the app.
- Withdraw consent & Request data deletion. (Note: Professional Audit Logs for Doctors may be retained as required by the Ministry of Health).
- Complain to our DPO (Data Protection Officer) if any data breaches are detected.
